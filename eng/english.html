1A place to begin

Welcome! You've arrived at the showcase and archive of my work — across time, place, and form.

I'm Edgardo Civallero: librarian, archivist, semantic consultant, musician, artist, writer, researcher, lecturer, professor, naturalist, memory worker, and epistemic dissenter — working from the high Andean foglands of Colombia.

This site gathers my projects in librarianship, archives, sound, visual art, ecology, and knowledge justice — where memory is woven with silence, where research meets rot, and where puppets argue with metadata.

Use the sidebar to navigate by theme. And if any thread resonates, write me. Otherwise, explore. You're already inside.
