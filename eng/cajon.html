Cajón de sastre (a Spanish expresion which literallytranslates as "a tailor's drawer" and that actually means "a mixed or grab bag, a catch-all") is an open-ended space where diverse topics converge, always anchored in the universe of libraries, knowledge, and memory. Here, unexpected explorations, unforeseen connections, and reflections that defy conventional frameworks illuminate new ways of thinking about information and culture.
