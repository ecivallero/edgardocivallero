This space presents two series of notes: The Letters from the Library and the Tips & tools for researchers (and everybody else). All of them have been produced in the scenario of my current workplace (Smithsonian Tropical Research Institute - STRI, 2024), have been published on the institutional platform of STRI library and archive ("Donde habitan las palabras"), and have a deep relationship with the disciplines of knowledge and memory management.

The entries will be listed below, following the chronological order of publication.
